About CLOIE

Discover the purpose and academic context of the capstone project.

CLOIE is a proposed department-level evaluation platform designed to support modern higher education. It facilitates the monitoring of Program Learning Outcomes (PLOs) and Graduate Outcomes (GOs) by gathering structured stakeholder feedback.

By bridging the gap between student outcomes and institutional objectives, this capstone project delivers a framework to support quality assurance, evidence generation, and continuous improvement for Assumption College of Davao.

🎓

Academic Excellence

Built to reflect purity in data and institutional devotion to educational improvement.

The Problem Context

The academic challenges CLOIE is designed to solve.

🧩

Fragmented Monitoring

Traditional learning outcome evaluation processes are often manual and scattered across different tools, making it difficult to gather cohesive, program-wide insights.

📄

Accreditation Evidence

Institutions face growing pressure to provide structured, clear evidence of outcome-based education and continuous quality improvement for accreditation.

💬

Stakeholder Feedback

Consolidating actionable feedback from alumni, employers, and faculty is challenging without a centralized system tailored to academic evaluation.

🔍

Quality Assurance

Academic leaders need organized reporting to conduct effective program reviews, identify curricular gaps, and drive institutional decision-making.

Key Features

Core capabilities of the proposed evaluation system.

🎯

Outcome Management

Streamlined tracking and management of Program Learning Outcomes (PLOs) and Graduate Outcomes (GOs).

📝

Standardized Tools

Deployment of standardized evaluation instruments across departments to ensure consistency.

🤝

Stakeholder Evaluation

Structured mechanisms for gathering targeted feedback from various academic stakeholders.

🔒

Data Privacy

Confidential, anonymized, and controlled data collection to protect respondent privacy.

📊

Evidence Generation

Automated reporting to synthesize evaluation data into clear evidence for program review.

📈

Continuous Improvement

Data-driven support for PDCA (Plan-Do-Check-Act) cycles to refine educational quality.
